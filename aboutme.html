What made me start a career in art?

Firstly, one of the great influences on my career in art is my mum. My mum is also an artist who also encouraged me to pursue my passion in the field of art. It's always nice to have someone who always supports me and is there to help me out if there is an issue I would be facing while I am working on a project of mine.

What is my passion for the art that I make?

COLOURS. The world is full of colours, which makes the whole world beautiful. Colour add personality to the object. The Colour also depict the cultural difference between the different regions, which in my opinion brings the world together. One of the top things for me to paint is nature. Nature art is always relaxing to draw and opens up a new perspective to view the world around us.
